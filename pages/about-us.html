About Launch Oral Systemic Health

Committed to become more... so we can give more.

The company was founded from a passionate belief that dentistry is a special gift to humanity and it's providers uniquely equipped and trained to deliver services that transform and alter lives. In addition, we have a certain responsiblility to attain our true potential as-well-as create meaningful and lasting relationships with our patients.

It is our mission to add value to the doctors and dental teams we serve... to do so with integrity, with excellent service, and with outrageous attention to detail.

We get excited about oral systemic health. As you know, oral systemic health has two components; supervising the systemic distribution of oxygen by the airway and supervising the systemic distribution of periodontal pathogens. Our focus and mission is limited to pathogen management and mitigation. Both are very important to human wellness and need to be part of an active therapy protocol we offer or refer our patients out for remedy.

Dr John graduated from Loma Linda University School of Dentistry in 1976 and has been engaged in clinical dentistry for almost four decades. In addition to post-graduate continuums in reconstructive dentistry, he has completed Dawson Academy's Dental Institute for Systemic Health and the medical Bale-Doneen preceptorship for cardiovascular health. As a certified professional coach, time is allocated annually to be with nationally renowned leadership. Dr John has spoken in behalf of oral systemic health across the country and been published in peer reviewed journals.

The personal side

John has served on school, health center, and charitable foundation boards over the years... giving back adds value and meaning to his life. He finds great joy spending time with family and friends... and can be found with a new book in his down time. Time spent outdoors is his opportunity to 'recharge'; tennis, golf, and hiking are all essential fundamentals to balancing life... but in the end, cross country 'bike' rides settle the spirit like no other!
